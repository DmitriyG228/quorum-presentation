# Advanced AI Society

- **Description:** Association of builders focused on proof of control technologies for the age of AI
- **Industry:** AI Safety, Trust Infrastructure
- **Involvement:** Advocating for human agency and control in AI systems

## Key Initiatives
- **Proof of control technologies:** Technical standards ensuring human authority and control over AI systems
- **Summit on Human Agency:** Scheduled February 23, 2025 (partnership with LF Decentralized Trust)
  - Establishing human authority as base layer in AI systems
  - Connecting trust infrastructure with AI agent governance

## Key Positions
- Human agency must be foundational—not added later
- All trust infrastructures must start with human authority
- Control should remain with human, not reside in AI itself
