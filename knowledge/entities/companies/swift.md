# SWIFT

- **Full Name:** Society for Worldwide Interbank Financial Telecommunication
- **Description:** Global financial messaging network for banks and financial institutions
- **Industry:** Financial Infrastructure, Payments, Messaging
- **Involvement:** Blockchain innovation to address stablecoin and digital asset concerns

## Key Initiatives
- **SWIFT ledger project:** Response to stablecoin and digital asset adoption threatening traditional bank deposits
  - Developed prototype with ConsenSys
  - Announced at Cyos Frankfurt conference (early 2024)
  - Moving rapidly toward implementation across multiple stages

## Context
- **Trigger:** Regional bank concerns about deposit flight to stablecoins (post-2023 banking turmoil)
- **Goal:** Enable traditional finance to participate in digital asset ecosystem on SWIFT's terms
- **Outcome:** "Permission" from big banks to move forward; treated as breakthrough by financial establishment
