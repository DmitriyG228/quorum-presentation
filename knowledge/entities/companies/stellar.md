# Stellar

- **Description:** Open-source blockchain platform designed for payments and remittances
- **Industry:** Blockchain, Cryptocurrency, Payments
- **Involvement:** Operates payment system using stablecoins for cross-border remittances

## Key Features
- Focus on financial inclusion and remittance corridors
- Stablecoin-based payment system reducing friction of international transfers
- Platform for issuance and trading of stablecoins

## Role in Stablecoin Ecosystem
- Exemplifies legitimate real-world use case for stablecoins: reducing remittance costs vs. traditional methods (Western Union, etc.)
- Demonstrates how stablecoins can serve underbanked populations
- Contrasts with speculative or DeFi-focused stablecoin use cases

## Use Cases
- **Remittances:** Wallet-to-wallet transfers of [[USDC]] to enable cost-effective international money transfers
- **Cross-border Payments:** Leveraging stablecoins to bypass traditional banking infrastructure and fee structures

## Policy Relevance
- [[Franklin Noll]] cited Stellar as example of practical stablecoin application for real economic need
- Remittance use case cited as one of few clearly legitimate current applications of stablecoins
