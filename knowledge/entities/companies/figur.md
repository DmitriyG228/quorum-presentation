# Figur

- **Description:** Blockchain-based mortgage and real estate asset tokenization platform
- **Industry:** Blockchain, Mortgage Finance, Asset Tokenization
- **Status:** Active
- **Focus:** Mortgage tokenization, HELOC tokenization, real world assets

## Business Evolution
- **Initial Focus:** Mortgage tokenization
- **Pivot:** Shifted to HELOC (Home Equity Line of Credit) tokenization as mortgage volume dried up
- **Current:** Semi-federally regulated HELOC market tokenization

## Strategic Positioning
Works in semi-federally regulated space (HELOCs) to avoid complex multi-agency approval requirements that would apply to conforming/conventional loan tokenization. This regulatory arbitrage allows faster deployment without SEC, CFPB, and GSE coordination.
