# Enterprise Ethereum Alliance

- **Description:** Industry consortium promoting Ethereum adoption in enterprises
- **Status:** Active
- **Involvement:** Early bridge between blockchain technology and traditional finance/enterprise

## Historical Role
- **Founding:** Joe Lubin and others founded EEA to bring blockchain to enterprises
- **Timeline:** Parallel to Hyperledger Foundation work (early-mid 2010s)
- **Mission:** Share decentralized trust innovations with enterprises

## Architecture Approach
- **Private/permissioned networks:** Initially focused on consortium and permissioned blockchain structures
- **Public blockchain foundation:** Built with understanding that public Ethereum would be underlying trust root
- **Compliance:** Addressed KYC/AML concerns from early stages
- **Members:** DTCC and other institutional actors participated

## Evolution
- **Early experiments:** Many projects attempted but faced institutional/technical challenges
- **Pivot:** Toward Layer 2 rollup architecture as solution for enterprise needs with decentralization
