# Brian Brooks

- **Title:** Official at Office of the Comptroller of the Currency (OCC)
- **Organization:** [[Office of the Comptroller of the Currency]]
- **Role in Community:** Regulatory advocate for stablecoin innovation

## Key Contributions
- September 2020: Issued interpretive letter allowing national banks to hold reserves for stablecoin issuers
- January 2021: Issued interpretive letter stating stablecoins can run on regular payment rails (independent node verification networks)
- Positioned stablecoins as equivalent to traveler's checks in regulatory framework
- Early regulatory voice supporting stablecoin infrastructure development
