# PayPal

- **Description:** Digital payments and fintech company
- **Industry:** Payments, Financial Services, E-commerce
- **Involvement:** Libra consortium member; exploring stablecoin payment options

## Key Events
- **June 2019:** Joined Meta's Libra consortium as founding member
- **2021-2024:** Investigating stablecoin payment integrations for user transfers

## Role in Stablecoin Ecosystem
- Initially co-endorsed Libra, later withdrew from consortium
- Positioned between traditional payment networks and crypto stablecoin ecosystem
- Exploring how to integrate stablecoin payments into existing user base

## Business Model
- Like [[Visa]], PayPal extracts transaction fees from its payment network
- Stablecoin integration could threaten existing fee model unless PayPal becomes stablecoin processor
- [[Franklin Noll]] notes potential for PayPal-like entities to issue corporate stablecoins with reward programs
