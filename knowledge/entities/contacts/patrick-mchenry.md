# Patrick McHenry

- **Title:** Member of US House of Representatives, House Financial Services Committee Chair
- **Organization:** [[United States House of Representatives]]
- **Party:** Republican
- **Role in Community:** House stablecoin regulatory champion, committee leadership

## Key Contributions
- Authored HR 4766 "Clarity for Payment Stable Coins Act"
- Key quote: "Stable coins hold the promise as a pillar of our 21st century payment system"
- Actively pushing for bill passage; resigning from Congress which motivated urgency on legislation
- Leading Republican effort to establish payment stable coin clarity as alternative to CBDCs

## Key Legislation
- HR 4766: Defines payment stable coin as digital asset with stable value, convertible to fiat, not a national currency or security
- Bill pending House floor vote as of May 2024 (date of meeting)
- Debate with Democratic opposition (Led by [[Maxine Waters]]) focused on regulatory regime (state vs. Federal Reserve control)
