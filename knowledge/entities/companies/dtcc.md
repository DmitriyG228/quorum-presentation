# DTCC

- **Full Name:** Depository Trust & Clearing Corporation
- **Description:** Central ledger for US financial services; custodian of equities and securities; settlement infrastructure
- **Industry:** Financial Services, Securities Settlement, Clearing
- **Involvement:** Leader in blockchain/tokenization innovation for traditional finance

## Key Facts
- **Assets under custody:** $100+ trillion in US equities
- **Market criticality:** US equities markets don't open without DTCC operations
- **Participants:** 300+ direct participants (banks, brokers, other financial institutions)
- **Ownership structure:** Unique governance model focused on industry benefit, not commercial profit (key to trust positioning)
- **Regulatory relationships:** SEC coordination; received no action letter (late 2024) for tokenization initiative

## Tokenization Initiative
- **Goal:** Enable tokenization of $100T in assets held in custody
- **Approach:** Multi-Layer 1 integration (not locked to single blockchain)
- **Compliance:** Creating "compliant-aware tokens" maintaining existing KYC/AML standards
- **Token movement:** Enabled only between registered wallets through DTCC service
- **Ecosystem participation:** Both DTCC's direct members and DeFi community can participate
- **Strategy:** Partnership-based expansion, not unilateral conquest

## Historical Evolution
- **2016:** Rob Palatnik begins blockchain exploration
- **2020s:** Increasing involvement with Hyperledger and enterprise blockchain efforts
- **2024:** SEC no action letter; major client inbound interest in tokenization
