# Hyperledger

- **Full Name:** Hyperledger Foundation (part of Linux Foundation Decentralized Trust)
- **Description:** Open-source blockchain project for enterprise applications
- **Industry:** Blockchain, Enterprise Infrastructure
- **Involvement:** Early collaboration point for DTCC and ConsenSys on permissioned blockchain

## Historical Role
- **Founding:** IBM announced Hyperledger Fabric at the very first Hyperledger meeting (circa 2014)
- **Michael Casey attendance:** Witnessed the original announcement and early evolution
- **Enterprise blockchain pioneer:** Initial focus on permissioned, private blockchain for enterprise use

## Evolution
- **Early 2010s:** Central to enterprise blockchain experiments (many failed)
- **Later:** Transitioned thinking toward public/semi-public blockchains and Layer 2 solutions
- **Current:** Part of "LF Decentralized Trust" umbrella; supporting both traditional and decentralized approaches
