# Joe Lubin

- **Title:** Founder, ConsenSys
- **Organization:** [[ConsenSys]]
- **Role in Community:** Ethereum ecosystem leader; advocate for decentralized trust and AI-protocol integration

## Meeting Appearances

## Key Contributions
- Co-founded Hyperledger Foundation's enterprise blockchain efforts
- Founded Enterprise Ethereum Alliance to bring blockchain to enterprises
- Pioneering rollup-centric architecture on Ethereum Layer 2 (declared "enterprise blockchain dead, long live enterprise blockchain")
- Collaborated with SWIFT to develop ledger prototype for post-stablecoin world
- Building decentralized AI agent protocols (ConsenSys, Ethereum Foundation collaboration)

## Key Positions
- **Decentralized trust foundation:** Loss of trust in centralized institutions created opportunity for cryptographic solutions (Bitcoin post-2008, Ethereum for general applications)
- **Enterprise blockchain evolution:** Private/permissioned blockchains failed; success comes from rollup-centric Layer 2 architecture inheriting Ethereum's decentralization and trust guarantees
- **AI and protocols:** Move foundational AI models to decentralized rails to avoid big tech centralization risks (paralleling social media polarization)
- **Agent infrastructure:** Building protocols for agents to pay each other, register capabilities, earn reputation through human feedback
- **80-year cycle theory:** Post-WWII debt super cycle ending; next era dominated by AI + decentralized protocols enabling real agency
- **Singularity:** Moving rapidly toward artificial super intelligence as hybridization of machine and biological intelligence

## Vision Details
- **Digital twin agents:** Everyone will have AI agent (tutor, mentor); feeds information, levels you up on any topic
- **Single controller:** Your agent has one main controller (you); represents you across Web2/Web3
- **Collective future:** Humans work in collectives with AI representatives and other intelligences
- **Information trust:** AI agent filters information, reducing polarity/fake news
- **Financial participation:** Tokenized assets (NFTs) with metadata enable agents to participate in finance, collateral, DeFi
