# Creator Assertions

- **Description:** Working group for content authenticity metadata and provenance
- **Status:** Active working group (joint venture)
- **Maintained by:** Joint effort between [[Trust Over IP]] and [[DCIF]]
- **Co-Chair:** [[Carla]]

## Purpose

- Support content authenticity and provenance
- Develop metadata standards for identifying and attributing content
- Integration with identity registries (IDER, HandID)

## Key Focus Areas

- **Identity Metadata:** Tagging identity information to content
- **DOI Integration:** Exploring DOI metadata insertion into [[COG]] (Content Object Graph?) specs
- **Registration Agents:** Working with major DOI registration agents (IDER, HandID)
- **Cross-Registry Integration:** Harmonizing different identity registry standards

## Related Registries

- **IDER:** Entertainment identity identifier registry
- **HandID:** Name, image, and likeness identifiers for individuals
