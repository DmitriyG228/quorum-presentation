# MISMO

- **Full Name:** Mortgage Industry Standards Maintenance Organization
- **Description:** Standards development and technology enablement organization for the mortgage industry
- **Industry:** Mortgage/Real Estate Finance, Financial Technology
- **Headquarters:** United States
- **Founded:** ~2000 (data standards); blockchain community of practice established 2018

## Organization Structure
- **Members:** 2,000+ organizations
- **Active Volunteers:** 580-600
- **Work Groups & Communities of Practice:** 39
- **Technology Focus Areas:**
  - Data standards (primary): Mortgage data model and specifications
  - Blockchain community of practice
  - AI community of practice (newest, established ~2023)
  - APIs (converting standards to usable interfaces)
  - Smart contracts (emerging)
  - Servicing standards

## Key Products & Standards
- **MISMO Data Model:** Foundational schema for mortgage data (~20+ years of development)
- **[[ELDD]]** (Electron Loan Data Dictionary): Modern evolution; boils data into unique identifiers and relationship mappings; enables transformation across different systems
- **API Toolkit:** Converts MISMO XML schemas into MISMO-compliant APIs; critical for blockchain interoperability
- **UPB Specifications:** Fannie Mae/Freddie Mac adaptations of MISMO model

## Key Initiatives

### Blockchain Community of Practice
- **Focus:** Education, standardization opportunities, interoperability solutions
- **Recent Work:** Servicing transfer prototype; interoperability prototypes using API toolkit
- **Education:** Lunch-and-learn sessions on decentralized identifiers, self-sovereign identities, blockchain fundamentals
- **Strategic Shift:** Moving from prototype-heavy to conceptual/theoretical work as infrastructure costs increase
- **Key Challenge:** Misconceptions persist; crypto mortgages vs. legitimate mortgage blockchain use cases

### AI Community of Practice
- **Launch:** Early 2023 (after ChatGPT release triggered industry interest)
- **Forums:** September 2023 (initial), June 2024 (San Francisco)
- **Participants:** AWS, Google, Rocket (lender), service providers, US Senate offices
- **Use Cases:** Document processing, developer assistance, chatbot enhancement
- **NOT Doing:** Autonomous underwriting without human-in-the-loop

## Collaboration with GSEs
[[Fannie Mae]], [[Freddie Mac]], [[Freddie Mac#Federal Home Loan Banks|Federal Home Loan Banks]], and [[Ginnie Mae]] are all active MISMO members:
- Each GSE assigns 6-10 representatives to MISMO summits
- Participation across multiple work groups and communities of practice
- GSEs maintain own specifications built on MISMO foundation

## Strategic Positioning
MISMO positions data standardization as the prerequisite for blockchain and AI adoption in mortgages. The path forward requires:
1. Trusted data ecosystem (via blockchain)
2. Clear data standards and interoperability (via ELDD and API toolkit)
3. Education across business and technology communities
4. Regulatory alignment and parallel development of regulatory solutions
