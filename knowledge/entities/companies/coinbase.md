# Coinbase

- **Description:** Cryptocurrency exchange and financial services company
- **Industry:** Cryptocurrency, Digital Finance, Payments
- **Involvement:** Operates stablecoin ecosystem; went public on NASDAQ in 2021; active in stablecoin policy discussions

## Key Events
- **2021:** Public listing on NASDAQ (IPO)
- **Stablecoin Involvement:** Platform for trading and use of stablecoins like USDC
- **Market Position:** Major exchange enabling retail and institutional use of stablecoins

## Role in Stablecoin Narrative Evolution
- Part of 2021 "crypto bull market" that drove narrative shift from "wildcat money" to "future money"
- Coinbase's NASDAQ listing symbolized mainstream acceptance of crypto infrastructure
- Growth of stablecoin volumes on platform contributed to Congressional perception of stablecoins as increasingly legitimate payment infrastructure
