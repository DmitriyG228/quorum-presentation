# Stacklock

- **Description:** Software company that originated the Minder supply chain security platform
- **Industry:** Cybersecurity, supply chain security
- **Involvement:** Founded and initially sponsored [[Minder]] project; mostly exited in 2025

## History

[[Stacklock]] founded [[Minder]] in 2023 as a supply chain security platform combining policy detection and remediation. In 2025, Stacklock largely exited the project while a few individual contributors remained. [[Evan]] (Minder lead) left Stacklock and has been leading Minder full-time as of 2025.
