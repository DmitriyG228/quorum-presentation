# Pat Toomey

- **Title:** US Senator
- **Organization:** [[United States Senate]]
- **Party:** Republican
- **Role in Community:** Stablecoin regulatory innovator

## Key Contributions
- Co-sponsored S.534 "Payment Stable Coin Act" with definition of payment stable coin:
  - Digital asset with stable value, convertible into fiat
  - Widely used as medium of exchange
  - Issued by centralized entity (not decentralized)
  - Does not pay interest (not a security)
  - Operates on a ledger (often distributed ledger technology)
- Framework adopted by subsequent Congressional stablecoin bills
- Bipartisan approach to payment stable coin regulation
