## Context

### Key Decision Points
- **Data-First Approach:** All participants agreed that mortgage data standardization (ELDD/APIs) is prerequisite for successful blockchain and AI adoption
- **Regulatory Parallelism:** Tokenization requires solving regulatory challenges in parallel with technology development, not as afterthought
- **Education Focus:** MISMO shifting toward conceptual/theoretical work and education as prototyping infrastructure costs increase

### Critical Unresolved Issues
1. **Regulatory Approval Path:** No clear pathway for conforming mortgage tokenization exists; requires multi-agency coordination (SEC, CFPB, Fannie Mae, Freddie Mac, Ginnie Mae)
2. **Federated AI Standards:** Discussed but not formalized; need standards for ethical data sourcing and compartmentalized federated learning in mortgage industry
3. **Interoperability Standards:** Multiple blockchain implementations will exist; standards for seamless interoperability via MISMO APIs/ELDD still being proven in prototypes
