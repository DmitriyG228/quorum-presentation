# Brian Steele

- **Title:** Executive Vice President (EVP), DTCC
- **Organization:** [[DTCC]]
- **Role in Community:** Leader of DTCC's digital assets and tokenization strategy

## Meeting Appearances
- [[2024-09-20-dtcc-consensys-conversation]] — Panelist discussing DTCC's blockchain evolution and tokenization initiative

## Key Contributions
- Driving DTCC's evolution from traditional securities settlement to tokenized digital assets
- Securing SEC no action letter (late 2024) providing regulatory certainty for tokenization
- Developing compliant-aware token approach maintaining KYC/AML standards
- Building multi-Layer 1 integration strategy to avoid lock-in to single blockchain
- Leading partnership approach to ecosystem development

## Key Positions
- **Market structure transformation:** Traditional financial services organized by asset class, geography, regulator; tokenization will democratize asset class and geography while maintaining regulatory structure
- **DTCC's role:** Leverage unique ownership/governance structure (not commercially motivated, focused on industry benefit) as foundation for trust in tokenized future
- **Trust as existential:** "If trust in what we do fails, there's no sense in having DTCC as an organization"
- **Partnership over conquest:** "It's not a DTCC conquers the world exercise"

## Strategy Details
- **Scope:** Tokenize $100+ trillion in US equities currently custodied by DTCC
- **Technical:** Tokens live in wallets of clients' choosing; activity occurs on any integrated L1
- **Compliance:** Bearer instruments avoided; same KYC/AML perimeter as today
- **Access:** Both direct members (300+) and DeFi actors can participate via existing participants
- **Regulatory:** Working in compliant, regulated manner with SEC coordination
