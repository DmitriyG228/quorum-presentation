# Visa

- **Description:** Global payment processing network and financial services company
- **Industry:** Payments, Financial Services
- **Involvement:** Member of Libra/Diem consortium; exploring stablecoin payment integration

## Key Events
- **June 2019:** Joined Meta's Libra consortium as founding member
- **2021-2024:** Exploring integration of stablecoins into payment rails; interested in capturing transaction fees

## Role in Stablecoin Ecosystem
- Initially seen as endorsement of Meta's Libra proposal, then withdrew
- Evaluating stablecoin adoption as potential future payment rail
- Potential to leverage existing merchant and acquirer relationships

## Financial Model
- [[Franklin Noll]] notes that current payment systems extract ~3% transaction fees
- Visa and similar networks benefit from transaction fee revenue
- Stablecoin payments could threaten this model unless Visa can become a processor of stablecoin transactions
