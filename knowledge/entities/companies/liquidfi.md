# LiquidFi

- **Former Names:** Liquid Mortgage, Liquid Mortgage Now (rebranded as LiquidFi)
- **Description:** Blockchain-based platform for tokenizing and trading mortgage assets
- **Industry:** Blockchain, Mortgage Finance, Asset Tokenization
- **Status:** Active
- **Focus:** Non-conforming mortgage tokenization, portfolio lending

## Business Model
- **Primary Focus:** Non-conforming lending (mortgages not backed by federal government)
- **Strategic Partner:** [[Redwood Trust]] (portfolio lending specialist)
- **Regulatory Approach:** Focus on private mortgage market where tokenized assets trade privately, avoiding complex federal agency approvals

## Regulatory Positioning
Like [[Figur]], LiquidFi uses regulatory arbitrage by focusing on non-conforming/portfolio lending rather than attempting to tokenize conforming loans (which would require SEC, CFPB, and GSE coordination).
