# Ethereum

- **Description:** Programmable blockchain platform enabling decentralized applications
- **Status:** Active
- **Maintained by:** [[Ethereum Foundation]], community

## Key Features
- **Smart contracts:** Programmable functionality beyond simple transfers
- **Layer 2 rollups:** Scaling solutions (optimistic and ZK rollups) enabling high throughput while maintaining base layer security
- **Decentralized consensus:** Proof of Stake enabling participation by many independent validators

## Role in Tokenization and Finance
- **Base layer:** Provides fundamental trust guarantees for rollup solutions
- **Integration point:** DTCC plans integration with Ethereum (one of multiple L1 networks)
- **Asset representation:** NFTs and ERC-20 tokens represent real-world assets (equities, commodities, etc.)
- **Metadata containers:** NFTs can carry complex metadata enabling programmable trading and collateral use

## Layer 2 Architecture
- **Rollup-centric future:** Ethereum is moving toward rollups as primary scaling mechanism
- **Enterprise usage:** Layer 2 rollups inherit full Ethereum decentralization while enabling diverse enterprise use cases
- **Cross-chain interop:** Interoperability protocols enable seamless activity across different rollup solutions

## Ecosystem
- **Enterprise Ethereum Alliance:** Formed to bring Ethereum to enterprises
- **ConsenSys:** Major developer on Ethereum layer 2 and protocols
- **Ethereum Foundation:** Core development and governance
