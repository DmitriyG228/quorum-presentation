# Ethereum Foundation

- **Description:** Non-profit organization supporting Ethereum protocol development and ecosystem
- **Industry:** Blockchain, Cryptocurrency, Decentralized Technology
- **Involvement:** Collaboration on decentralized AI protocols and agent infrastructure

## Key Activities
- **Protocol development:** Core Ethereum technical research and upgrades
- **Ecosystem support:** Grants and support for projects building on Ethereum
- **AI protocol collaboration:** Working with ConsenSys on decentralized agent infrastructure
  - Agent-to-agent payment protocols
  - Capability registration systems
  - Reputation mechanisms with human feedback

## Current Focus Areas
- **Layer 2 scaling:** Supporting rollup development and interoperability
- **AI and decentralization:** Exploring intersection of decentralized protocols and AI agents
- **Enterprise adoption:** Enabling Ethereum for traditional finance use cases
