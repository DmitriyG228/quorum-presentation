# Project Guardian

- **Description:** Collaborative initiative exploring decentralized finance and asset tokenization in wholesale funding markets
- **Status:** Active (started May 2022)
- **Maintained by:** [[JP Morgan]] (lead), [[DBS Bank]], [[SBI]], [[Wisdom Tree]] (consortium)
- **Focus:** Institutional-grade DeFi products, asset tokenization, wholesale market innovations, open and interoperable networks

## Key Objectives
1. Develop institutional-grade decentralized finance products
2. Explore asset tokenization in wholesale funding markets
3. Create open and interoperable network infrastructure
4. Investigate trusted infrastructure solutions for wholesale finance

## Initiatives
- **Money Authority of Singapore Pilot:** JP Morgan conducted pilot program with Monetary Authority of Singapore, turning insights into tangible products
- **Wisdom Tree On-Chain Services:** Unified interface for diverse on-chain services; integrating tokenized portfolios and trading banking services
- **Money Market Fund Tokenization:** Exploring tokenized money market funds with stablecoin integration for diversification and risk management

## Consortium Members
- [[JP Morgan]] — Lead institution
- [[DBS Bank]] — Asia-Pacific presence, institutional banking expertise
- [[SBI]] — Global financial institution, DeFi engagement
- [[Wisdom Tree]] — Financial services and on-chain capabilities
