# ConsenSys

- **Description:** Ethereum infrastructure company building decentralized protocols and enterprise solutions
- **Industry:** Blockchain, Web3, Decentralized Finance
- **Involvement:** Pioneer of enterprise blockchain, AI protocols, and TradFi-DeFi convergence

## Key Focus Areas
- **Enterprise blockchain:** Transitioned from permissioned/private chains to Ethereum Layer 2 rollup-centric architecture
- **AI and decentralized protocols:** Building infrastructure for AI agents to operate in trustless environments
  - Agent-to-agent payments
  - Capability registration and discovery
  - Reputation systems with human feedback mechanisms
- **Financial interoperability:** Bridging traditional finance and DeFi through technical standards and partnerships

## Historical Involvement
- **Hyperledger Foundation:** Co-founded early enterprise blockchain efforts
- **Enterprise Ethereum Alliance:** Established to bring blockchain to enterprises (with multiple partners)
- **Consensus conference:** Major industry gathering on decentralization and crypto
- **SWIFT collaboration:** Developed ledger prototype responding to stablecoin banking concerns

## Current Initiatives
- **Decentralized AI protocols:** Working with Ethereum Foundation on agent infrastructure
- **Ethereum Layer 2 ecosystems:** Rollup-centric architecture as foundation for enterprise and AI applications
- **SWIFT integration:** Ongoing ledger development for traditional finance

## Key Philosophy
- "Enterprise blockchain dead, long live enterprise blockchain" — Transitioned from failed private chains to public Layer 2 rollups maintaining decentralization guarantees
- Rollups inherit full trust of base Ethereum layer while enabling diverse use cases
- AI and decentralization are dominant technologies of next economic super cycle (emerging from 80-year post-WWII debt cycle)
